We store game progress, settings, and achievements locally on your device using secure local storage mechanisms. This data includes:

  • Game settings and preferences

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the game functionality
  • To save your game progress and settings
  • To personalize your gaming experience

  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize gameplay
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

Your game data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure SharedPreferences system. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
